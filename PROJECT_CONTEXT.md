# RepoDepot — Core Definition & Standards

## Product Definition (Immutable)
**RepoDepot** is a curated marketplace for complete, deployable software repositories that can be purchased and immediately used, modified, or monetized by the buyer.

### What We Are
- A marketplace for finished, production-ready software businesses
- A transaction platform (Stripe integration)
- A delivery mechanism for full IP ownership transfer
- A supply-quality curator that rejects low-value code

### What We Are NOT
- A repository intelligence platform
- An audit or scanning tool
- An analysis engine (SLOC counts, quality scores, dependency trees)
- An onboarding SaaS
- A sync or intelligence product

## Marketplace Primitives
1. **Listings**: Business value descriptions, ROI breakdown, tech stack, price, deployment readiness
2. **Discovery**: Browse by use case, filters, categories (no AI "intelligence")
3. **Transactions**: Stripe payment processing with 15% platform commission (85% to seller)
4. **Delivery**: Instant secure handover—GitHub transfer, zip, or access handoff

## Quality & Business Focus
- Curate finished, valuable products only
- Zero tolerance for code dumps or half-built templates
- Every decision must serve transaction success and buyer trust
- Emphasize ownership transfer, zero strings attached

## Target Users
- **Buyers**: SaaS founders, digital agencies, technical entrepreneurs
- **Sellers**: Developers with finished, sellable products ready to monetize

## Core Features (MVP)
- Curated listing gallery with business outcome focus
- Production-ready checkout (Stripe Checkout Sessions)
- Instant delivery mechanism
- Seller submission form for curation pipeline
- Trust signals: Full IP ownership, instant delivery, 30-day guarantee

## Success Metrics
- Conversion rate on checkout
- Number of completed transactions
- Seller supply quality
- Buyer retention & repeat purchases

## Forbidden Language
Remove or replace anywhere in the codebase:
- "SLOC", "Code Quality", "Audit", "Dependency Tree", "Quality Score"
- "Intelligence", "Analysis Engine", "Scanning"
- "Smart" features, "AI-powered recommendations"
- "Onboarding sessions", "sync"
