# RepoDepot Core Mandates

## Product Definition
A curated marketplace for complete, deployable software repositories that can be purchased and immediately used, modified, or monetized by the buyer. RepoDepot is **NOT** a repository intelligence platform or AI analysis tool.

## Marketplace Primitives
- **Listings**: Business value descriptions, screenshots, tech stack, price, deployment readiness.
- **Discovery**: Browse by business use case, filters, categories.
- **Transactions**: Stripe integration with a 15% platform commission.
- **Delivery**: Instant secure handover of repository access (GitHub transfer/zip/access handoff).

## Quality & Business Focus
- **Supply Quality**: Prioritize curation of finished, valuable products. Low-quality code dumps are strictly prohibited.
- **Liquidity & Conversion**: Every decision must serve transaction success and buyer trust.
- **No Bloat**: Avoid AI analysis, repository intelligence, SLOC counts, quality scores, or complex onboarding sessions.

## Architecture
- **Frontend**: Visual impact is critical. Maintain the "battle-tested" aesthetic but focus on business outcomes.
- **Backend**: Focused on transaction security and delivery automation.
