# RepoDepot Core Mandates

## Product Definition
RepoDepot is a curated marketplace for complete, deployable software repositories. It is **NOT** a repository intelligence platform or AI analysis tool.

## Marketplace Primitives
- **Listings**: Metadata, screenshots, tech stack, price, deployment status.
- **Discovery**: Browse, filters, categories.
- **Transactions**: Stripe integration with a 15% platform commission.
- **Delivery**: Secure handover of repository access (GitHub transfer/zip/access handoff).

## Quality & Business Focus
- **Supply Quality**: Prioritize curation and verification. Low-quality code dumps are strictly prohibited.
- **Liquidity & Conversion**: Every decision must serve transaction success and trust.
- **No Bloat**: Avoid AI analysis, repository intelligence, or complex onboarding SaaS features.

## Architecture
- **Frontend**: Visual impact is critical. Maintain the "battle-tested" aesthetic.
- **Backend**: Focused on transaction security and delivery automation.
